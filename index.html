About Me

My passion for computer science started at age 12 with a simple Flappy Bird clone. In high school, I earned my CompTIA A+ certification and gained hands-on experience with networking and hardware. I also volunteered with my church's Production team, learning how different technologies work together seamlessly.

At California Baptist University, I've joined the VEXU Robotics Team as a software engineer, programming systems that integrate mechanical and electrical subsystems in competitive settings. I've also completed a summer internship at the CBU AI Machine Learning Lab as a Salesforce/Agentforce Developer, designing AI-powered applications and agents. My expertise spans full-stack development, AI & machine learning, object detection, mobile app development, and Salesforce automation.

I'm passionate about building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. Whether it's autonomous robotics, web applications, or AI agents—I'm committed to creating technology that makes an impact.
